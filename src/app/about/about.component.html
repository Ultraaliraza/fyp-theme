About Us

The social network, on which people can discuss their society issues, Upload videos and photos, text or PDF files, send and receive messages and comment on An issue.

This website will solve social issues in social sectors and this system help Peoples to solve problem, discussing with counselors and share their issues on Application. Identifier will identify a major problem in our society. Councilors provide services for no financial or social gain to benefit another group or organization. After mentioning about the introduction of the software system, the specific requirement will be addresses for it. In the final part, functional and non-functional requirement will be addressed.

Our Vision

Our organization is a social network that connects peoples. Our Mission is to provide information to the users about social issues and peoples who have issue. Identifier will share social issues and problem in our society. He can upload issue, upload videos, upload photos, chatting with other users etc. Moreover, users can create social groups on issues like Universities issues, education issues, marriage issues, poverty issues and other issues in our society. Counselors will guide identifiers on their issues and communicate with them directly. Volunteer can raise funds for financial issues and communicate with identifier. It is a social network that connects peoples and peoples can use this system for their social problems.

The main purpose of this project is to solve social issues in our society, raising awareness in peoples, providing counseling to peoples, show right path to peoples, raise funds for financial issues, improve quality of human right, to bring reform, to change thinking of people etc. In addition to these, it provide a basis for validation and verification
